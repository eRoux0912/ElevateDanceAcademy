ELEVATE Dance Academy

Welcome to ELEVATE Dance Academy

ELEVATE Dance Academy has been one of the West Coast's most passionate dance schools for over 10 years. We believe dance is more than movement — it is confidence, discipline, and self-expression. From our first class to our latest production, we have dedicated ourselves to building dancers who perform with heart.

Why Choose ELEVATE?

Choosing the right dance academy shapes more than just technique — it shapes character. At ELEVATE we offer a supportive, professional environment where students of all ages and skill levels are challenged to grow. Our experienced instructors bring dedication and energy to every class, ensuring that every student leaves feeling more capable than when they arrived.

Our Classes

Ballet

Ballet is the foundation of all dance. At ELEVATE we offer classical ballet training for all ages, focusing on technique, posture, and grace. Whether you are a complete beginner or looking to refine your skills, our ballet classes build strength and elegance from the ground up.

Hip Hop

High energy and full of attitude, our Hip Hop classes bring street style into a structured and fun environment. Students learn rhythm, coordination, and freestyle movement while building confidence and developing their own unique style on the dance floor.

Contemporary

Contemporary dance blends elements of ballet, jazz, and lyrical movement into a fluid and expressive art form. Our contemporary classes encourage students to connect emotionally with movement, pushing creative boundaries while developing technical control and body awareness.

Modern

Modern dance is where creativity meets structure. Rooted in breaking away from traditional forms, our modern classes teach students to explore movement freely while building core strength, flexibility, and performance quality that translates across all dance disciplines.

About ELEVATE

ELEVATE Dance Academy was born from a simple belief — that every person deserves a space to move, express, and grow. For over a decade we have been more than a dance school. We are a community where beginners find their footing, and seasoned dancers find their voice. Everything we do is driven by passion, professionalism, and a deep love for the art of dance.
